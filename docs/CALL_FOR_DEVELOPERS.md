# BPIP — Call for Developers

## This Is a Call to Builders

BPIP is not a startup demo.
It is not a hackathon project.
It is not a speculative crypto experiment.

BPIP is an attempt to correct a structural failure of the internet:

> **Those who build the digital world rarely participate economically in the value it generates.**

If you have ever:
- contributed to open source and received nothing,
- paid expensive infrastructure bills just to keep building,
- helped companies scale without sharing in the upside,
- believed the internet could be fairer to its builders,

then this call is for you.

---

## What BPIP Is Trying to Build

BPIP is a **protocol-driven economic infrastructure** that transforms:

- real digital work  
- real system usage  
- real infrastructure contribution  

into **verifiable economic participation**.

Not promises.  
Not reputation points.  
Not “maybe someday”.

Participation is encoded as a **rule of the system**.

---

## What Makes BPIP Different

BPIP does not reward:
- hype
- speculation
- passive holding
- early insiders

BPIP rewards:
- impact
- contribution
- maintenance
- real usage
- long-term responsibility

If no value is generated, nothing is distributed.

This is non-negotiable.

## How Contribution Works in BPIP

BPIP does not use goodwill as currency.

Every accepted contribution:
- is validated
- is measured by impact
- is recorded as an event
- increases your economic participation

Participation is **earned**, not granted.

Details are defined in:
- `docs/CONTRIBUTION.md`
- `docs/ECONOMY.md`
- `docs/PROTOCOL.md`

Read them. They matter.
